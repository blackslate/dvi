##You open the page and see:

* Title
* Instructions (IKEA-style) 
---
* A sentence with a gap
* The verb to use in the gap
* Place for a video. This actually shows ???
* A progress bar, to show how many questions have been answered (correctly)
* A tab at the bottom of the screen. Pressing this...
    - opens a table of conjugations
* (Optional)
    - Contextual menu for verbs
    - Contextual menu for persons


##Action 
* Type into the gap (text input)
* Open conjugation table and drag word
 
##Reactions
* As word is typed, if input is impossible, latest letter is removed
* When input contains correct string, video plays

##Background actions
* Video is pre-loaded
* Sentence is chosen at random from a list
